Inexperienced Fortune Teller

This is Owen the fortune teller. Say hello!

Owen has invited you for a personal reading. He can't stress enough that this reading is on the house! (he did not go to wizard school for a degree in economics, this is for sure.)
Owen's ears aren't the best with old age, so he can only answer a very slim arrangement of questions, but his answers are as specific as it gets for fortune tellers.
His crystal ball hasn't been used in a while so who knows what answer he'll get. On the bright side, it shouldn't turn you into a toad! so why not give it a try?
